Mathematical Modelling

Optimal Spacing of Transverse Bars

A mathematical model to determine the effective positioning of yellow transverse bar markers on UK motorway slip-roads to encourage driver deceleration.

Context:

Yellow lines on road approaches provide visual and auditory cues to drivers, encouraging deceleration.

Objective:

Design a spacing sequence for maximum deceleration effect.

Features:
  • Linear model with constant deceleration aligned closely with government specifications.
  • Model enhancement with a 'thinking time' parameter showed finer alignment.
  • Variable driver acceleration suggests potential for model expansion to include variable deceleration phases.
